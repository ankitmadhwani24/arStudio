METHODOLOGY

Agile software development

At ArStudioz, we work with an Agile Approach, called Scrum Methodology. This works by splitting the development work in sprints of about 1.5weeks. This means developing the product incrementally, allowing you to add any changes that may be needed and adapting the app on the go, while getting potentially shippable products after each increment. This systematized approach has worked well for us.
